Rent Increases
Most renters in Ontario are protected from large annual increases by the rent control rules set under the RTA.
Buildings built or first occupied before November 15, 2018
Your landlord can generally only increase your rent once a year by a set percentage, as per the rent increase guidelines under the RTA.
In 2024, the maximum your landlord can increase your rent is 2.5 per cent.
Buildings built or first occupied after November 15, 2018
Your unit is exempt from the rent increase guidelines.
Your landlord can increase your rent by any amount a maximum of once per year.
